Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-05

This Privacy Policy describes how MU LOST&FOUND ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application MU LOST&FOUND (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Account Data: if you register or sign in, we may collect a username or email address.
  • Profile & Post Data: photos, descriptions, item details and the approximate location you provide when posting lost or found items.
  • Communications: messages you send and receive through the App's in-app messaging system and any contact preferences you provide.
  • Device & Usage Data: device model, OS version, app version, unique device identifiers, analytics, crash reports, and usage statistics (to improve the App).
  • Optional Data: any data you choose to include in a post (e.g., reward offered, additional contact details). Do not post extremely sensitive personal data (see Section 8).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features (posting/searching lost or found items, map features, messaging).
  • Enable communication between users to facilitate item recovery.
  • Personalize content and search results based on location and preferences.
  • Diagnose technical issues and analyze usage to optimize performance.
  • Send important notices (e.g., changes to policies, safety alerts) if you opt in.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (If applicable)

If your jurisdiction requires a legal basis for processing personal data, our lawful bases include:

  • Consent: when you opt into location sharing, push notifications, or other optional features.
  • Performance of a contract: providing the service you requested, such as listing an item.
  • Legitimate interests: improving the App and preventing fraud, while balancing your rights and privacy.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: the content you post (photos, descriptions, approximate location) is visible to other users so they can help identify and return items.
  • Service providers: third-party vendors who help us operate the App (analytics, crash reporting, hosting). They access only the data necessary to perform their services and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requests: to comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests, or protect rights, property, or safety.
  • Business transfers: in connection with mergers, acquisitions, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate protections.

5. Third-Party Services & Links

The App may use third-party services (for example analytics, or cloud storage). These services have separate privacy policies and may collect data independently. We encourage you to review their privacy practices.

The App may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as needed to provide the App and for legitimate and necessary purposes, such as complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements. Posts (lost/found listings) will remain visible until you remove them or they expire per our retention policy.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to retention requirements).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at lostandfoundotpservice@gmail.com. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

8. Safety, Moderation & Sensitive Data

Please do not post extremely sensitive personal information (e.g., national ID numbers, bank account details, health records). If you accidentally include sensitive data in a post, contact us immediately so we can assist.

We encourage safe meetups and recommend neutral public locations when arranging returns. The App does not take responsibility for user-to-user interactions outside the App.

9. Security

We employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. Please take care when sharing personal details in your posts and messages.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of the policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
